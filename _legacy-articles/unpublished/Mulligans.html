Mulligans Your odds of holding Power and Influence increase when you redraw your opening hand
To get a better understanding of how Eternal's redraw rules shape the performance of your deck's Power base, we can identify patterns using data from a Monte Carlo simulator
In a typical deck at the start of the game, your odds of holding single Influence in any one faction (F, T, J, P, S) increase by a difference of roughly five percent after a redraw. This difference shrinks incrementally by about one percent with each additional draw.
